It's Fine. a series

A dark comedy following talented but under-employed playwright Mackenzie Barker as she pays the bills catering to New York's elite, all while surviving her sociopathic coworker.

Sarah Mack "/&" Lauren Dortch-Crozier were finished waiting around for permission to tell stories.
They vowed to take their artistic paths into their own hands, to work with as many powerful women they could, and to make people laugh through their own personal stories.
It's Fine was successfully funded in record time in August and wrapped filming late November 2015.
